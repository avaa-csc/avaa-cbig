On this web site, you can explore the results of the analyses carried out by the Conservation Biology Informatics Group of the University of Helsinki. Results of two extensive analyses are presented: one exploring the global protected area network expansion (GPAN) and other exploring the priority areas for global carnivore conservation (Carnivores). The results have been published in scientific journals on 2014 (GPAN in Nature) and 2016 (Carnivores in Scientific Reports).

  • Global protected area network expansion analysis (GPAN)
  • Global carnivore conservation prioritization (Carnivores)

The site allows you to explore the results of these analyses through slideshow presentations and interactive maps and graphs. Additionally, you may download the results of these analyses as spatial data and statistics. These web sites were created by Open Science and Research Initiative AVAA team and members of the Conservation Biology Informatics Group of the University of Helsinki
